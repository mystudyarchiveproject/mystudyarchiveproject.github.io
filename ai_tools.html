revaldoai.com

flashcards & questions

revaldoai.com is a website to make instant flash cards, quizzes and makes an AI study plan based on your field of choice it also has an ai chatbot and uses the feynman technique to explain topics.

1. upload the data
2. choose required length for flashcards and level of hardness
3. download them and use the same data to make quizzes
4. publish on our site!

homework help

solvely.ai is a website used to help students in homework in all subjects including writing an essay, it can also transform audio or data into simplified notes.

1. log in and upload the data
2. specify the field your notes should be based on and enter the preferred amount of flashcards to be generated
3. download them and upload to the site
